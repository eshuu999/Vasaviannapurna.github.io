Our Story & Trust

Where Every Meal Is Crafted with Love

At Vasavi Annapurna, we believe food is more than nourishment — it is tradition, warmth, and belonging. Rooted in the heart of Rajampet, our kitchen brings together the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh with flavors that span North Indian, South Indian, and Continental cuisine.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen passes through the hands of experienced chefs who treat each plate as a promise — a promise of freshness, flavor, and care. We source the finest ingredients locally, ensuring every bite carries the essence of quality and authenticity.

A Word From The Founder
Managing Director's Message
"At Vasavi Annapurna, quality is not a policy — it is a habit we practice every single day. From the moment our kitchen fires up in the morning to the last meal we serve at night, every ingredient is chosen with care, every dish prepared with pride. We believe that trust is built one plate at a time, and it is that trust that has made our guests our family. When you dine with us, you are not just a customer — you are a guest in our home. I personally ensure that the values of purity, hospitality, and excellence are upheld in every corner of our restaurant. Come hungry, leave happy — that is our heartfelt commitment to you."
LN
Likhit Narasimha Ch
Managing Director, Vasavi Annapurna
